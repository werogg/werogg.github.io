ESL Winter Season 2019
Snow is falling, temperatures are going down. The Winter is coming! Time for a new ESL Season as well! You can decide weather running an Open League or a Seasonal Tournament or even both! Get prepared, work on a concept and run some winter tournaments in your game, edition and region!
Besides the normal Seasonal events there is the possibility to host a X-Mas-Special-Cup. The frame conditions can be found here in this separate News.

As always every game, every edition and every region is welcome running some Winter tournaments! Here is a short comparison between both concepts and all details on the upcoming season!

    Procedures

    Open Leagues
    • If your game must host an Open League the first coordination actions take place between CM and the respective GSH
    • In any case the GSH gets his (regional) Staff Heads involved
    • After the frame conditions have been sorted the tournament is enrolled
    • Send in a support ticket to Cup Squad (Global), including all needed information (links, schedule...) & possible exceptions which have been approved by your CM
    • After everything was checked for the last time by the Cup Squad you can announce & start your tournament
    Seasonal Tournaments
    • All seasonal tournaments must be approved by the Cup Squad beforehand!
    • Send in the request via support ticket to Cup Squad (Global)
    • The ticket must contain the following:
      • Name, format, signup period + links, start date, unpublished newsposting
      • desired Prize-Packages

    Winter Schedule

    The following schedule is a rough orientation. There should not be any problem for the Seasonal Tournaments to follow those dates. There can be bigger discrepancies regarding the Open Leagues due to being dependent on Major-/Pro-Leagues.
    • 30th December: Requesting Deadline
    • Floating: Public Announcement News (Country-Portals/Global)
    • 10th March: End of all Winter Events
    • 1 Week of possible delays/season break (17th March; )

    Need help running your tournament?

    Got great ideas for this, but already a lot of plans? Can't you be available on certain dates and therefore can't host this cup? Want to provide players a nice time with this cup, but you don't have the staff to handle it?
    No worries! The Game Operations squad can help you out! Create a help request before announcing and they'll see if they can support your cup!
